The youngest club among the greats of Brazilian football, São Paulo FC is the most winning club in Brazil!

Founded on January 25, 1930 and marked by stories of overcoming, such as the club's re-foundation in 1935 or the difficult times of building Morumbi, Tricolor Mais Querido on the planet is the only Brazilian club to have conquered América and the World for 3 times. He is also the only consecutive three-time champion of the Brazilian Championship and the first to win it six times. Brazilian three-time champion.

Off the field, Tricolor defied critics and logic and built the largest private stadium in Brazil, Estádio do Morumbi. It continued to be a pioneer in its commitment to infrastructure as a pillar of excellence in sport, building its Training Center and Athlete Training Center over time. The result? The world met three times.

Owner of an enviable heritage, holder of unattainable titles for many and in constant movement in search of excellence, Tricolor Paulista lives up to its anthem: among the greats, you are the first!
