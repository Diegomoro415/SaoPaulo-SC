Our facilities

Inaugurated on July 16, 2005 - São Paulo's golden year - our soccer camp is yet another pioneering initiative. Built on a plot of 50,000 square meters, the center is an international reference in the work of training athletes. With an excellent sports structure, the modern facilities also have an excellent educational and administrative structure, highly functional and of extreme comfort and tranquility for young people who dream of pursuing a career in sports, or simply practicing sports for leisure.

At the training center, the U-20, U-17, U-15, U-13 and U-11 categories of Tricolor train. All the athletes of the São Paulo base receive not only sports but also pedagogical, psychological, nutritional, medical, dental, social assistance and press assistance. In addition, São Paulo FC maintains agreements with schools in the region, guaranteeing young people's access to education and monitoring the school performance of each athlete, who has individualized assistance and tutoring at their disposal.
